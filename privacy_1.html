MOXIS ONE

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026 · Version 1.0
The short version: Moxis One collects only what it needs to work — your email, your selected health goals, and your scan activity. We never sell your data and we show no ads. Product details and your goals are processed by an AI service to generate your verdict. You can permanently delete your account and personal data yourself, inside the app, at any time.

1.Who We Are

Moxis One ("Moxis One," "we," "us") is a product-scanning app that lets you scan food, supplement, and beauty products and receive a personalized, AI-generated verdict based on the health goals you select. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to the Moxis One mobile app.

2.Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address and basic profile details such as a display name.

Health goals. During onboarding you select the goals you want verdicts personalized to (for example, clean eating or muscle building). These are stored with your account. Your goals are preferences you choose — Moxis One does not collect medical records or health measurements.

Scan activity. When you scan a product, we record the product's barcode and label information (such as name, brand, and ingredients), the AI verdict generated for it, and when it was scanned. Scan history associated with your account is used to power features like your history and dashboard.

Disclaimer acceptance. We record the date, time, and version of the health disclaimer you accepted. We keep this so we have an accurate record of the terms you agreed to.

Basic technical data. Our infrastructure provider (Google Firebase) automatically processes standard technical information needed to operate the service, such as authentication tokens and request logs.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers. We do not use third-party advertising or marketing trackers.

3.How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized product verdicts based on your selected goals.
  • To operate app features such as scan history, search, and your dashboard.
  • To maintain your account and keep the service secure.
  • To keep a record of the disclaimer version you accepted.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4.AI Processing

When you scan a product, the product's name and ingredients, together with the goals you selected, are sent to OpenAI through our secure server to generate your verdict. This processing happens under OpenAI's API terms. The verdict returned is stored with the product record so it can be shown to you again. AI-generated content can be inaccurate — verdicts are informational only and are not medical advice.

5.Services We Rely On

Moxis One is built on a small number of service providers, each receiving only what it needs:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — account authentication, database, and server functions. Your account data and scan history are stored here, on servers located in the United States.
  • OpenAI — receives product name, ingredients, and your selected goals to generate verdicts, as described in Section 4.
  • Open Food Facts — a public product database we query to identify scanned products. It receives only the product barcode, never your personal information.

6.Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure in the United States and is encrypted in transit and at rest by that infrastructure. Access credentials for AI processing are held server-side in a managed secret store and are never included in the app itself. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we design the service to minimize the data we hold and where it can travel.

7.Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your personal information for as long as your account exists.

You can delete your account yourself, inside the app (Coach page → Delete Account). Deletion is permanent: it removes your login account, and our servers then automatically delete your profile data, including your goals and disclaimer record. For security, deletion requires a recent login — if your session is older, the app will ask you to sign in again first.

General product records created by scanning (a product's barcode, name, and ingredients) describe the product, not you. They may remain in the shared product catalog after your account is deleted, with nothing linking them to you.

8.Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information. You can exercise deletion directly in the app at any time, and you can view or change your goals in the app. For anything else — including access or correction requests — contact us at the address in Section 11 and we will respond.

9.Children

Moxis One is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

10.Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version here and update the effective date. For material changes, the app will present the updated terms for your acceptance before you continue using it.
